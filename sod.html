Inspired by The Caretaker " Everywhere At The End Of Time." a 6 hours long album by James Leyland Kirby. His piece was exploring dementia with disfiguring 1920's music at each stage worsening to the next. I wanted to visualize exploring the world of dementia to feel the nostalgic retro style of the 1920s and distorted music in that timeline. It is a slow, painful process of losing oneself to a point, not knowing anything, forgetting everyone and themself. This project is to give an understanding of the experience of having dementia. This disease is not often discussed, to sympathize, family members who have dementia or experiencing themselves.
