About me


Hey, I'm Ziad, a graphic designer talented in UX/UI design/ Motion Graphics/ Illustration. I focus on creating visually pleasing designs with clean functionality of the interface. Besides web building, I love to do storytelling to bring it all together.

I've always loved the idea of animations when I was young and still do as it can gather different types of emotion to people's hearts or just creating short unique motion graphics that seem neat.
